Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

1. Introduction

IQWorld ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and IQ test services. By using IQWorld, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide the service:

  • Anonymous Data: IP address, browser type, device info for analytics (no personal identification).
  • Test Data: Your answers (anonymous, not stored).
  • Cookies: Session cookies for functionality; no tracking cookies.
No email, name, or personal data required.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate IQ scores and generate certificates.
  • Improve service (aggregate, anonymous analytics).
  • No selling or sharing with third parties (except legal requirements).

4. Google Ads Compliance

We use Google Ads for promotion. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits. We do not share personal data.

  • Opt-out: Visit Google Ads Settings or YourAdChoices.
  • Third-party vendors (including Google) use cookies to serve ads based on prior visits. No device ID tracking.

5. Cookies & Consent

We use only essential cookies to:

  • Save test progress
  • Prevent abuse
  • Ensure smooth experience

No analytics. No tracking. No third-party cookies.
You see a consent banner on first visit. Decline = test still works.

6. Data Security

We use HTTPS and secure servers. No data stored long-term. Compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and COPPA.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

IQWorld is not for children under 13. No data collection from minors. Parents: Contact us if concerned.

8. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Access, delete, or correct data (email hello@iqworld.com).
  • Opt-out of any future data use.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this policy. Check back periodically. Last updated: November 4, 2025.
